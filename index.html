featured places

Blue Eye, Saranda

Blue Eye Albania is a stunning natural wonder, featuring crystal-clear blue water surrounded by lush greenery.

Albanian Alps, Albania

The Albanian Alps are a breathtakingly beautiful mountain range, known for their rugged peaks, deep valleys, and pristine alpine scenery.

Sazan Island, Albania

Sazan Island is a small, rocky island located in the Adriatic Sea off the coast of Albania, known for its strategic military importance and historic significance.

Gjirokastra, Albania

Gjirokastra is a charming historic town in southern Albania, famous for its unique Ottoman-style architecture, cobbled streets, and stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

Kruja Castle, Albania

Kruja Castle is a medieval fortress located in the city of Kruja, Albania, and is an important historical and cultural site, known for its association with Albania's national hero, Skanderbeg.

Tirana, Albania

Tirana is the capital and largest city of Albania, located in the center of the country, known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and unique blend of architectural styles.
